Our Programs

Three Pathways to Literacy Superstardom 🚀

Each J Phonics program is carefully crafted to meet your child exactly where they are — and take them further than they imagined.

J Phonics Core Method

Powered by Synthetic Phonics

All three of our programs are grounded in a structured synthetic phonics system — teaching children how individual sounds map to letters so they can confidently decode words they've never seen before. This approach builds genuine reading independence right from the very beginning.

🔤
Level 1 · Foundation

Phonics Fundamentals

Sounds are the building blocks of reading. This program gives children a rock-solid start — step by step, sound by sound — so they grow into readers who can tackle anything with confidence.

1
Letter Sounds
Recognising & producing the sounds of the alphabet
2
Phonemic Awareness
Training ears to hear and identify sounds in words
3
Blending & Segmenting
Reading by joining sounds; spelling by breaking words apart
4
Digraphs & Letter Combos
Discovering how letters team up to make new sounds
5
Phoneme Manipulation
Adding, removing & swapping sounds to build new words

📚
Level 2 · Accelerated

J Phonics Program

Going further with structured, explicit phonics — this program develops the reading accuracy, fluency, and spelling independence children need to handle both familiar and brand-new words with ease.

1
Sound-First Learning
Understanding how letters represent sounds to build accuracy
2
Blending to Read
Confidently decoding words by combining individual sounds
3
Segmenting to Spell
Breaking words into sounds for accurate, independent spelling
4
Reading Fluency
Developing speed and flow for smooth, expressive reading
5
Literacy Independence
Especially supportive for early learners and those needing extra guidance

Level 3 · Expression

Grammar & Writing

Once children can read, it's time to find their voice. This program explores how language works — from words and phrases to clauses and sentences — empowering children to write with clarity, purpose, and creativity.

1
Language Structure
Understanding how words, phrases & clauses combine
2
Grammar Foundations
The essential rules that make communication clear and accurate
3
Sentence Craft
Moving beyond simple sentences to varied, expressive writing
4
Creative Writing
Planning, drafting, and sharing original stories and ideas
5
Purposeful Communication
Writing with intention, voice, and real-world confidence
